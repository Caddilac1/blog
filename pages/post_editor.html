Web design has evolved dramatically over the past decade, with user expectations reaching new heights and technology advancing at breakneck speed. Whether you're a seasoned designer or just starting your journey, understanding these fundamental principles will help you create websites that not only look stunning but also provide exceptional user experiences.

1. Mobile-First Design

With over 60% of web traffic coming from mobile devices, designing for mobile first isn't just a best practice—it's essential. Start with the smallest screen size and progressively enhance for larger displays.

2. Visual Hierarchy

Guide your users' eyes through your content using size, color, contrast, and spacing. The most important elements should be the most prominent, creating a clear path for users to follow.

3. Consistent Typography

Choose a maximum of 2-3 fonts and use them consistently throughout your site. Establish a clear hierarchy with different font sizes and weights for headings, subheadings, and body text.

Remember, great design is not just about making things look pretty—it's about solving problems and creating meaningful connections between your brand and your users.
